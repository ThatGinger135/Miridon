Overview

Led by Baron Crinne, Harbant is the second-largest town in Miridon

Harbant is a thriving city filled with crafts of all sorts. It is the place to come to get something built, carved, smithed, or created to the very highest of quality.

People

There are all sorts of people here with many different professions. The most skilled craftsmen in Harbant are often very competitive with each other.

Places of Interest

Craftsman's Square

Sitting at the centre of Harbant, Craftsman's Square is a bustling area filled with shops and stalls.
