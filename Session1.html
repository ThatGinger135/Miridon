Session 1: The Quest Begins

Date: January 1, 2023

Summary

The adventurers meet in a tavern and are hired by a local noble to retrieve a stolen gem from a nearby dungeon. They encounter goblins and traps, but ultimately succeed in finding the gem and returning it to the noble.

Notes

  • Party consists of: John (Human Fighter), Jane (Elf Rogue), Mike (Dwarf Cleric), and Sarah (Halfling Wizard).
  • Adventure takes place in the town of Oakhurst, located in the kingdom of Khorvaire.
  • Noble who hires the party is named Lord Alderleaf.
  • Goblins are guarding the entrance to the dungeon, which is located in a nearby forest.
  • Dungeon contains traps, such as a pit trap and a poison dart trap.
  • Party finds the stolen gem in a hidden chamber at the end of the dungeon.
