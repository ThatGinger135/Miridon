Overview

Home to King Edward of Miridon, Oberfell is certainly the richest place in Miridon.

While most people will travel to Harbant to spend their money, the rich individuals will source the work they want form the idividuals they want it from across the continent.

People

The people of Oberfell are rich, and it is home to the primary residance of the royal family
