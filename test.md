::: {#overview .section}
### Overview

Barrow-By-The-Water is a fishing village that exports a lot of fish. It
has a good trade with Lexan and the other cities.
:::

::: {#people .section}
### People

The people of the town all seem to be somewhat superstitious about the
moon and tides.
:::
